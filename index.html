Germanshepherd

This breed originated from Germany as it is named.It is also known as alastian or GSD.It is medium to large in size which is about 65cm.It is a longcoated dog that means it has a lot of fur in it's body which is mostly black,white,(black and silver),(black and tan),(red and black),sable and gray in color. The dog weighs about 22-40kgs depending on the gender. It is inteligent,obidient,loyal and watchful.The dog is mainly used by policeofficers and the military to sniff around and it is also used by blind people to guide them as they walk around.It was originally bred as a herding dog for herding sheep.

Rottweiler

The dogs were known in German Rottweiler Metzgerhund, meaning Rottweiler butchers' dogs, which is where they originated from.The dog is regarded as medium-to-large or large in size with a broad head,short fur and wide set of eyes.They are members of the working group.It's height is about 69cm and weighs between 35-60kgs also depending on the gender.It is fearless,steady,self-assured and alert.Mainly it's used as pollice dogs,personal protectors and also distinguish themselves as search and rescue dogs.In the anciet times it was mainly used to herd livestock and pull carts laden with butchered meat to market.

Kangal

The Kangal Shepherd Dog is a traditional Turkish breed of large livestock guardian dog which got it's name from a district known as kangal.It is known of because of it's cropped ears and it's height is about 80cm.It weighs between 40-60kgs depending on the gender.it's color is dun,light-golden,wolf-stable,fawn and grey.The dog has a temperament of being alert,protective,calm,self-controlled and sensitive.It is a traditional flock guardian dog, kept with flocks of sheep to fend off wolves,cheetahs and other predators.Even now they are used as personal protective dogs and for herding.


Bulldog

The dog is beleived to have originated from United Kingdom.Also known as english bulldog or British bulldog.They are medium-sized,muscular dogs of around 40-55 Ib. They have large heads with thick folds of skin around the face and shoulders and a relatively flat face with a protruding lower jaw.They are mostly (fawn and white) ,(red and white) in color.They weigh between 18-25kgs. Bulldogs are good pets and function well in apartments and homes without a yard.They are affectionate,docile,sometimes aggresive,willful,friendly and gregariousThey are called bulldogs because they wereknown for bullbaiting.

Chihuahua

A chihuahua is a toy dog believed to have it's origins at Mexico named after a state in Mexico.It is usually kept as a companion dog or show.It is short haired or long haired and unicoloured.It's size is about 23cm and weighs about 1-3kgs.Tey are mainly kept as pets because they love playing and are meant to stay under one roof with human not outside.Due to their size and weight they do have a lot of enemies one being eagles,dogs that are larger than them and small children.Another enemy for them is thunderstorms as they frighten them.These dogs are high maintainance as they have to be groomed by trimming the fur.

Caucasian Shepherd

It is believed to have originated in Russia.The Caucasian Shepherd Dog is also called Caucasian Ovcharka.They have a height of 75cm and have a weight of 36-70kgs.They are stronger than wolves.It is indeed considered one of the most powerful dog breeds.They are strong,powerful,quick,alert and dominant. Health

Spina bifida in dogs is a congenital condition that occurs when a dog’s spine and spinal cord fail to develop properly during early gestation. This condition belongs to a group of disorders known as neural tube defects. The neural tube is the embryonic structure that eventually develops into a dog’s brain, spinal cord, and the tissues that enclose them. When part of the neural tube fails to close all the way, the result is spina bifida. As such, it is a relatively rare condition, primarily occurring in the lumbar or sacral regions of the spine.
